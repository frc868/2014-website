TechHOUNDS is willing to accommodate any daily (prescription or nonprescription) medication to be taken at the event that your child may require. Note that any pre approved medication to be administered on the day of the event will be given by a CHS teacher. On the day of the event, the parent must deliver the medication in person to the designated individual in a sealed bag with the child’s name, parent’s name, type of medication, amount of medication, and time to administer medication.
